About

I'm a recent graduate of the University of Pittsburgh’s Frederick Honors College, where I studied political science and criminal justice with minors in professional writing and information science.

I care deeply about building systems that are accessible and ready to meet the challenges of the digital age. Whether I’m creating materials for a criminal diversion program, developing frameworks for ethical data governance, or conducting policy research, my belief in the power of interdisciplinary problem-solving and informed design remains central to my work.
